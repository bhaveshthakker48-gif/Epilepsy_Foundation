The aim and intention of Epilepsy Foundation India, in its efforts and also in this Epilepsy Foundation app, is the health, treatment and well being of all patients, especially the rural poor across the length and breadth of India.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our website and mobile app, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, phone number, and email address. The information that we collect will be used to contact or identify you.

The data of Epilepsy Foundation application is saved on the server in encrypted format for security purposes. Government recognized Aadhaar card and voting card are used for patient identification, since these are unique identity documents mandated by law.

Permission is taken for camera and gallery in the application so that image of duly signed patient consent form, taken before admitting the patient for eye surgery as well as implant label details of the implant fitted to the patient during cataract surgery are captured and stored. This is a medico-legal requirement under laws in India. These images are captured in the eye pre-operation notes and surgical notes sections of this application.

Also, the patient's health related data, primarily medical history, collected by this application is used for taking detailed patient health history which is a must for proper treatment.

Patient registration for the medical treatment is done through the website and there is no age barrier for offering the free treatments and medical awareness given during Epilepsy Foundation Indias ground level work in rural areas.
